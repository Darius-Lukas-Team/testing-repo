2

Rate each task on repeatability, data availability, and impact. Focus on the top 20% of tasks that consume 80% of your team's time. These are your highest-ROI automation targets.
3
Deploy AI Agents One at a Time
Start with your highest-scoring process. Build the AI agent, test it for 2 weeks, then move to the next. Each agent connects to your central system, creating compounding leverage.
4
Monitor, Measure, and Optimize
Set up dashboards to track each agent's performance. Review weekly. Optimize prompts and workflows monthly. Your system gets smarter and faster over time — automatically.
