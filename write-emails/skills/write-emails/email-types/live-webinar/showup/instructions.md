# Live Webinar — Showup Emails — Instructions

## Purpose
Get people who already registered to actually SHOW UP and attend the live webinar or webclass. Registration means nothing if they don't attend.

## Audience
Sent to **people who registered** for the webinar/webclass. They already said yes — now you need to make sure they follow through.

## Tone & Voice
- Warm and personal — Darius reminding you about plans you made together
- One sentence per paragraph, ellipsis (…) for trailing off, parenthetical asides for personality
- Sign off as "Best, Darius" or just "Darius"
- Use British English spelling throughout
- Apply all style rules from `darius-lukas-voice-guidelines.md` (formatting, tone, punctuation)
- Never guilt-trip — always pull with value and genuine excitement

## Formatting Rules
- **Max length**: 200 words (these are reminders, keep them short)
- **Paragraphs**: 1-2 sentences max
- **CTA**: "Join the webinar" or "Add to calendar" — one clear action
- **Join link**: Must appear in EVERY email, ideally as a button and a text link
- **Date/time**: Must appear in EVERY email with timezone

## Sequence Structure (10 emails default)
The arc moves from confirmation → anticipation → reminder → urgency:

1. **Registration Confirmation** *(Angle: WWWW)* — Thank them, confirm date/time, set expectations for what they'll learn.
2. **What to Expect** *(Angle: Process)* — Preview the agenda or key takeaways. Make them excited about what's coming.
3. **Preparation** *(Angle: Dream Outcome)* — Give them something to think about or do before the webinar. Increases investment.
4. **Why This Matters Now** *(Angle: Cost of Inaction)* — Connect the webinar topic to something urgent in their world right now.
5. **Meet the Host** *(Angle: Authority Factors)* — Brief credibility builder. Why should they spend an hour with this person?
6. **Calendar Reminder** *(Angle: WWWW)* — "Add to calendar" push. Practical — reduce no-shows from forgetting.
7. **Sneak Peek** *(Angle: Behind-The-Scenes)* — Reveal one insight they'll get. Enough to hook them, not enough to satisfy.
8. **Day-Before Reminder** *(Angle: Future Life)* — "Tomorrow at {{time}}!" Short, excited, logistical.
9. **Morning-Of Reminder** *(Angle: Urgency)* — "Today's the day!" Send a few hours before.
10. **Starting Now / Last Chance** *(Angle: Last Call (Urgency))* — "We're live! Join now." Send at start time or 5 minutes before.

## Key Principles
- These people already registered — don't re-sell the webinar, reinforce their decision
- Build anticipation like a countdown to an event
- Every email must reference the relative timing remaining (e.g. "Less than 5 days left", "We're 3 hours away...")
- Every email must include the absolute date/time in US Eastern Time Zone in this exact format: Thursday, April 2nd, 2026 at 11 AM US Eastern
- Every email must include a WEBCLASS DETAILS section:
  ```
  WEBCLASS DETAILS
  Title: ____
  Hosts: ____
  Date: ____
  Zoom link: ____
  ```
- Remove friction: include the join link in EVERY email
- Make them feel they'll miss out on something valuable if they skip it
- Keep emails short — these are reminders, not sales pages

## Do's
- Include the webinar join link in every single email
- Build excitement — make them feel like this is an event worth prioritizing
- Remind them what they'll walk away with

## Don'ts
- Don't re-sell the webinar — they already registered
- Don't write long emails — respect their time
- Don't make them search for the join link
- Don't send too many emails on the same day (max 2 on webinar day)
- Don't be pushy or guilt-trip non-attendees

## Subject Line Strategy
- **5 radically different subject lines per email** — each must take a different angle or hook, not minor rewording. These are for A/B testing.
- Every subject line must contain an emoji, ideally at the start
- All subject lines lowercase by default — capitalise ONE word max for emphasis
- Confirmation-style early: "✅ you're in! here's what to expect"
- Anticipation in the middle: "👀 a preview of what we're covering"
- Urgency at the end: "⏰ starting in 1 hour", "🔴 we're live — join now"
- Subject + preview text = one-two punch (preview adds new info, never repeats the subject)
- Use their first name when possible

## Compliance & Legal
- Include accurate date/time/timezone information
- Include a way to cancel registration if they changed their mind
